AI in Real Estate: work smarter, not longer.

Short, practical lessons built for busy agents. No fluff. Copy‑paste playbooks you can use today.

Module 1: Lead Generation with AI

Capture leads everywhere: website, IG/FB, listing portals, QR codes at open houses. Normalize the data, tag by intent, and push to your CRM.

  • JotForm/Typeform → Google Sheet/Airtable
  • Auto‑tag: buyer/seller, price band, timeline
  • Duplicate check + merge

Result: cleaner pipeline, faster response, higher conversion.

Module 2: AI‑Powered Follow‑Ups

Instant replies within minutes, personalized with name, neighborhood, and price range. Sequences stop on reply and hand off to you.

  • SMS for speed, email for depth
  • Voice agent for after‑hours
  • Auto‑log notes back to CRM
Module 3: Appointment Scheduling

Let prospects pick a time that works. Sync to Google Calendar, add buffers, and send reminders + reschedule links.

  • Auto‑time‑zone detection
  • No‑show reducer: T‑24h SMS
  • Block double‑bookings
Module 4: Listing Descriptions with AI

Feed the property facts; get MLS‑safe copy + social captions. Keep tone on‑brand with a saved voice profile.

  • Facts > fluff: beds, baths, upgrades, HOA
  • Bullet + paragraph variants
  • Accessibility and Fair Housing safe language
Module 5: CMA + Market Reports (Assist)

Compile comps, trends, and a clear narrative. Automate the repetitive parts; keep your expert judgment.

  • Monthly report emails
  • Buyer/seller tailored insights
Module 6: CRM Cleanup & Tagging

Deactivate stale leads, update tags by behavior, and surface re‑engagement opportunities automatically.
